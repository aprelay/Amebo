## üé® Creating Ad Banners

### Free Tools:
1. **Canva** (Recommended)
   - Go to: https://canva.com
   - Search: "Banner ad 320x100"
   - Use free templates
   - Download as PNG/JPG
   - Upload to your website or image host

2. **Placeholder.com** (Quick testing)
   - Format: `https://via.placeholder.com/320x100/COLOR/TEXTCOLOR?text=Your+Text`
   - Example: `https://via.placeholder.com/320x100/E91E63/FFFFFF?text=Sale+50%+Off`

### Design Tips:
- **Size:** 320x100 pixels (recommended)
- **Colors:** Use your brand colors
- **Text:** Keep it short (max 5-7 words)
- **CTA:** Include action word (Shop, Follow, Visit, Get)
- **Contrast:** High contrast between background and text
